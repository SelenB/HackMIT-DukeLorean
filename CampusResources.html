    • Advice for Choosing Fall Courses

    1. Understand your requirements.

      Notice that every first-year must be taking either a writing or seminar course. If you are taking a writing course, the letter W will be noted in your DukeHub account. Otherwise, please choose a seminar course.

    2. Choose multiple classes that interest you.

      Smaller classes, such as seminar and writing courses, may often fill up quickly depending on its popularity. Make sure that you bookbag a few classes just in case your first choice is full.
